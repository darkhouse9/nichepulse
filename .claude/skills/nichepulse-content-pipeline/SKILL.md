# Content Pipeline Skill

Transform a single topic or keyword into a complete multi-platform content package.

## Trigger

Use when user asks about:
- Content creation, drafting, or ideation
- Social media post creation
- Blog post outlines or drafts
- Multi-platform content strategy
- Content calendar creation
- Repurposing content across platforms

## Workflow

### Step 1: Define the Content Brief

Gather from user (or infer):
- **Topic/Keyword**: The core subject
- **Target Audience**: Who is this for (demographics, interests, pain points)
- **Goal**: Awareness, engagement, conversion, or authority
- **Platforms**: Which platforms to create content for
- **Tone**: Professional, casual, witty, educational, inspirational

### Step 2: Research Competing Content

Use `web_search` to find:
- Top-ranking content on this topic
- Viral social posts about it
- Common angles and approaches
- Gaps and underserved angles

### Step 3: Generate Content Package

Produce content for ALL requested platforms:

**Blog Post:**
- SEO-optimized title (under 60 chars)
- H2/H3 outline (8-12 sections with key points)
- Meta description (under 155 chars)
- Intro paragraph (hook, promise, preview)
- Key statistics or data points to include
- CTA suggestions

**X/Twitter Thread:**
- Hook tweet (controversial, surprising, or question-based)
- 5-8 thread tweets with specific copy
- Closing tweet with CTA
- Hashtag suggestions (3-5)

**LinkedIn Post:**
- Professional angle on the topic
- Personal story hook OR data-driven insight
- 3-5 short paragraphs with line breaks
- CTA for engagement ("Agree? Disagree? Share your experience")

**Instagram/TikTok Caption:**
- Hook in first line (before "more" button)
- 2-3 short paragraphs
- 5-10 relevant hashtags
- CTA for saves/shares

**Email Newsletter:**
- Subject line (3 options)
- Preview text
- 3-5 paragraph body with personal tone
- CTA button text + link description

### Step 4: Schedule & Repurpose Plan

Output a repurposing schedule:

```
Day 1: Publish blog post
Day 2: X thread (summarize 3 key points)
Day 3: LinkedIn post (personal angle)
Day 4: Instagram carousel (extract key stats)
Day 6: Email newsletter (roundup format)
Day 14: Republish updated X thread with new hook
Day 21: Turn into video script outline
```

## Quality Standards

Every piece of content should:
1. Lead with a hook (surprise, question, bold claim, or relatable pain point)
2. Be specific (numbers, examples, named things — not vague generalities)
3. Have a clear CTA (what should the reader do next?)
4. Match the platform's native format (not cross-posted verbatim)
5. Include ego-bait when appropriate (tagging relevant accounts, quoting sources)

## Platform-Specific Rules

**X/Twitter:**
- Threads over 5 tweets need a strong hook AND payoff tweet
- Questions get 3x more engagement than statements
- Quote-tweet format works for commentary on trending topics

**LinkedIN:**
- First-person stories outperform advice posts
- Line breaks every 1-2 sentences (readability)
- Tag 2-3 relevant people in the first 20 mins for distribution

**Instagram:**
- Carousels with 5-7 slides perform best
- Saves > likes for algorithm boost
- Text-overlay images with key quotes perform well

**Email:**
- Subject lines: under 50 chars, curiosity or urgency
- Preview text should complement (not repeat) subject
- Single CTA, above the fold if possible
